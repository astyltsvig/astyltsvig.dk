## Background

The insurance and pension sector needs secure digital surfaces where both corporate clients and end users can access their policies. Off-the-shelf tools did not cover the specific need, and the complexity of the data required bespoke solutions.

## Brief

Develop and operate secure customer portals for SecureLife and Care Pension og Forsikring (CPOF), where both brands could offer access to policies through their respective domains.

## Approach

The first generation of the portal was developed and has been in daily production since 2022. A next-generation portal is in development on a fresh platform with a modernised architecture, ready to take over when it launches.

Focus has been on:

- An architecture that accommodates today's operation as well as the upcoming migration
- Stable, high-reliability operation for a business-critical system
- A solution designed to evolve and be developed further as needs change over time

I am responsible for development, architecture, and operations on both platforms.

## Outcome

A portal in daily production since 2022 and a next-generation platform in active development. The engagement is a multi-year relationship covering the full lifecycle — operation, maintenance, and modernisation.
